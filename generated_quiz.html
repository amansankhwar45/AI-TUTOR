Interactive Quiz

Question 1

What is the value of x in the equation 2x + 5 = 11?

A. 2
B. 3
C. 4
D. 5
CORRECT ANSWER
B. 3
To find the value of x, we need to isolate x on one side of the equation. Subtracting 5 from both sides gives 2x = 6, and then dividing both sides by 2 gives x = 3.

Question 2

What is the shape with four right angles and four sides of equal length called?

A. Square
B. Rectangle
C. Triangle
D. Circle
CORRECT ANSWER
A. Square
A square is a quadrilateral with four right angles and four sides of equal length. This is the defining characteristic of a square, making it the correct answer.

Question 3

What is the result of 6 - 3 + 2?

A. 3
B. 4
C. 5
D. 6
CORRECT ANSWER
C. 5
To evaluate this expression, we need to follow the order of operations. First, subtract 3 from 6 to get 3, and then add 2 to get 5.

Question 4

What is the fraction 1/2 equivalent to as a decimal?

A. 0.2
B. 0.5
C. 0.8
D. 1.0
CORRECT ANSWER
B. 0.5
To convert the fraction 1/2 to a decimal, we divide the numerator by the denominator. 1 divided by 2 equals 0.5, making this the correct answer.

Question 5

If a pencil is 15 cm long and it is divided into 5 parts, how long is each part?

A. 1 cm
B. 2 cm
C. 3 cm
D. 5 cm
CORRECT ANSWER
C. 3 cm
To find the length of each part, we divide the total length of the pencil by the number of parts. 15 cm divided by 5 equals 3 cm, making this the correct answer.
